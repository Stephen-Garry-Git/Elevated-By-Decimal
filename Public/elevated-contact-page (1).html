Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What services does Elevated by Decimal offer?

We offer comprehensive digital marketing and technology solutions including web development, SEO services, social media management, content writing, creative design, automation engineering, and code templates. We're a one-stop shop for all your digital needs.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A basic website takes 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive digital marketing campaigns can take 3-6 months for full implementation. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation phase.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer flexible pricing models including project-based, monthly retainers, and hourly rates depending on your needs. Contact us for a free consultation and custom quote tailored to your specific requirements and budget.

Do you work with businesses outside Nigeria?

Absolutely! While we're based in Lagos, we work with clients across Africa and globally. Our remote-first approach allows us to deliver exceptional results regardless of your location.

What makes Elevated by Decimal different?

We combine technical expertise with creative excellence and local market knowledge. Our team understands the African market while delivering international-standard solutions. We're committed to transparency, data-driven strategies, and measurable results.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes! We offer various support and maintenance packages to ensure your digital assets continue performing optimally. From website updates to ongoing SEO and marketing support, we're here for the long term.
